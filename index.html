Juggling with Typography

This further progresses by incorporating typography into the mix, paving the way for what could eventually become a generator of circus posters for street performers.
'Circo Semáforo' thus represents a unique blend of permanence and ephemera, embodying a festival of performances that occur daily in the urban landscape. Interactive posters, drawing inspiration from traditional circus poster art, exist within modern digital media as a novel means of advertisement for this spectacle.
